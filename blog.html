The QXUVUS Program

On August 1, a few anonymous citizens of Bakenstown have started a program known as "QXUVUS". Their goal is, quote, "to bring happiness to people." They have been running public events with Vaughny Jamerick as their speaker. They've run many talks on happiness and philosophy, during which they sell shirts to raise money for these events and also are developing plans to become an anti-depression charity.

In an interview with Vaughny, he tells that the members have plans to expand to more towns in the area, eventually growing to the whole country. He states, "Our goal is to help people reach fulfillment. To ascend beyond the mundane that is society and to reach true happiness. That is our one goal."

If you wish to support them, they are running their events at the Bakenstown Community Center over the weekends from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM.
